Search Engine Optimization

Become that One in a Billion

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) means working to get a website to appear high up in the search results for one or several keywords. A key element of SEO is to choose keywords that are both relevant to your website and high in search volume, increasing the chance of generating relevant traffic to your site. SEO can be divided into on- and off-page optimization. The former focuses on the work to optimize your website whilst the latter focuses analyzing the links to your site from external sources.
